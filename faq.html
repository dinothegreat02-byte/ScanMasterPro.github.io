Frequently Asked Questions

What is ScanMasterPro?

ScanMasterPro is an online QR and document scanning platform that works directly in your browser.

Is ScanMasterPro free to use?

Yes, the platform is free to use.

Do I need to install any software?

No, ScanMasterPro works directly in modern web browsers.

Can I use it on mobile devices?

Yes, the website is mobile-friendly and works on smartphones and tablets.

Does ScanMasterPro store my scans?

No, we focus on privacy and do not permanently store your scanned data.
